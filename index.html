Measure around your natural waistline, just above your belly button, without pulling the tape tight.

Our story

Made by hand, made in Kerala

Dreams In Vogue started with Keerthy's love for traditional Kerala craftsmanship — the gold zari of a kasavu saree, the patient stitch-by-stitch of aari embroidery, and the quiet pride of a blouse that fits exactly right.

"Every order is treated like it's for someone in my own family — that's the only way I know how to do this."

What began as custom blouse stitching for friends and family has grown into a full boutique — maternity wear, sarees, hand-embroidered aari work, and fully custom outfits — while keeping the same hands-on, made-to-measure approach it started with.

Have something specific in mind?

Tell us the fabric, the fit and the occasion — we'll take care of the rest. Every custom order is stitched around your measurements.
